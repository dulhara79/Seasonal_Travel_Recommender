Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour with Travling? +
You can book through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our customer service team. We offer instant booking confirmation and flexible payment options.
What's included in your travel packages? +
Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, entrance fees, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by package - check individual tour details.
Do you provide visa assistance? +
Yes! We provide complete visa assistance including ETA applications, document preparation, and guidance through the entire visa process for Sri Lanka.
What's your cancellation policy? +
We offer flexible cancellation with full refund up to 48 hours before travel. For later cancellations, fees may apply. Premium members get enhanced cancellation benefits.
Do you offer customized tours? +
Absolutely! Our AI travel planner and expert team can create personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and travel preferences. Contact us to start planning your custom adventure.
